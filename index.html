About Us

According to Census 2022 information the location code or village code of Kesharpura village is 510109. Kesharpura village is located in Idar taluka of Sabar Kantha district in Gujarat, India. It is situated 18km away from sub-district headquarter Idar (tehsildar office) and 35km away from district headquarter Himatnagar. As per 2009 stats, Kesharpura village is also a gram panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 294.54 hectares. Kesharpura has a total population of 5,980 peoples, out of which male population is 3,105 while female population is 2,875. There are about 1076 houses in kesharpura village. Pincode of kesharpura village locality is 383230.
